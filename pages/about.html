About Me

Hi! I’m Denise — a Combinatorics & Optimization student at the University of Waterloo, graduating in April 2028. I’m happiest when I’m building things: data tools that reveal patterns, optimization models that make decisions smarter, and front-end projects that feel delightful to interact with. Math gives me structure; coding lets me create; and design ties everything together.

When I’m not studying or experimenting with new ideas, I’m out skiing, playing badminton, or fencing my way through stress. I’m excited about meeting new collaborators, tackling meaningful problems, and growing into the kind of developer who mixes logic with creativity. Hope to see you around!
